How to Play Shape Sorceress

Welcome to the enchanted world of Shape Sorceress, where you'll use your magical powers to identify and sort shapes based on their special properties! This guide will help you understand how to play the game and become a true Shape Sorceress.

What is Shape Sorceress?

Shape Sorceress is a magical adventure where you'll discover the enchanting properties of different shapes. Your task is to identify shapes based on their special qualities (like number of sides, symmetry, or angles) and sort them into the correct magical containers.

Magical Shapes:

You'll encounter various enchanted shapes with different properties. Your task is to sort them based on the magical property shown at the top of your spell book.

Example magical properties: 3 sides 4 equal sides Perfect roundness Mirror magic

How to Play:

  • When you start the adventure, you'll see a magical property displayed at the top of your spell book.
  • Enchanted shapes will appear on your magical canvas.
  • Use your wand (mouse or finger) to drag the shapes that match the magical property into the glowing cauldron.
  • If you place a shape in the wrong cauldron, it will gently float back to the canvas.
  • If you correctly sort all the shapes with the target property, your spell will be complete and sparkles will fill the screen!
  • The adventure becomes more magical with each level, introducing new shape properties and more complex sorting challenges.

Magical Tips:

  • Take your time to observe each shape's special qualities before sorting.
  • Learn to recognize the magical properties of shapes - like the number of sides, whether all sides are equal, and types of symmetry.
  • Look for special sparkles around shapes that might give you clues about their properties.
  • Collect magical gems as you progress through the levels to unlock special powers!
  • If you're not sure about a shape, you can use your "Reveal Magic" power (once per level) to highlight all shapes with the target property.
