How to Play Puzzle Paradise

Welcome to Puzzle Paradise, a magical garden where numbers bloom and grow! This guide will help you understand how to play the game and make your garden flourish with beautiful math.

What is Puzzle Paradise?

Puzzle Paradise is a number puzzle game where you need to fill in missing numbers to make each row and column add up to a special magic sum. By completing the puzzles, you'll help your magical number garden bloom!

How to Play:

  • When you start the game, you'll see a grid with some numbers already filled in.
  • Click on any empty cell to select it.
  • Then click on a number from the number pad to place that number in the selected cell.
  • Your goal is to fill in all the empty cells so that each row and column adds up to the magic sum (usually 15).
  • Once you've filled in all the cells, click the "Check Garden" button to see if your solution is correct.
  • If all rows and columns add up to the magic sum, your garden will bloom with magical sparkles!

Garden Growing Tips:

  • Look at the numbers that are already filled in and think about what numbers you need to add to reach the magic sum.
  • If a row already has two numbers, you can figure out the third by subtracting those two numbers from the magic sum.
  • For example, if a row has 8 and 1, and the magic sum is 15, then 15 - 8 - 1 = 6, so you need to place a 6 in the empty cell.
  • Remember that each number can only appear once in the garden!
  • If you make a mistake, you can click on a cell and then click the "C" button to clear it.

Math Skills You'll Practice:

  • Addition (adding numbers in rows and columns)
  • Subtraction (figuring out what number is needed to reach the sum)
  • Problem-solving (finding the right combination of numbers)
  • Logical thinking (using clues to determine where numbers should go)

Controls:

  • Click on an empty cell to select it
  • Click on a number in the number pad to place it in the selected cell
  • Click the "C" button to clear a selected cell
  • Check Garden button verifies if your solution is correct
  • Next Level button takes you to a more challenging garden
  • Start Over button lets you begin again from the first level

As you complete each level, your garden will grow more beautiful and the puzzles will become more challenging. You'll earn magic points for each level you complete!
